Our Services

"Our pet care offers a range of services to cater to your furry friend's needs."

Overnight Pet Care

We'll have one of our highly-trained and background-checked sitters take care of your pet's food, water, bathroom, and exercise needs later in the evening.

Pet Taxi

Our pet taxi can make sure your pet safely gets where they need to go, whether it be a vet appointment, trip to the groomers, or playdate at the park.

Medical Administration

Our medical administrators are experienced with pills, shots, injections, and can ensure that your pet is getting the right medication at the right time.

Puppy Sitting

Our local sitters can help you customize the perfect feeding, exercise schedule for your pup while helping them learn the proper way to behave in your home.

Playtime and Socialization

We'll engage your cat in playtime activities, such as laser pointers, feather toys, and scratching posts, to keep them entertained and exercised.

Pet Grooming

Our groomers will provide nail trimming, bathing, and haircuts for your pet, teeth brushing, ear cleaning and keeping them clean and well-groomed.

  • When you ask care for your pet, we collect information about your pet, age, breed, and health information.
